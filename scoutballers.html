About ScoutBallers

Scoutballers is a football marketplace that connects players, video creators, and recruiters to hasten the process of club and college recruitment. The platform will help professional clubs and college coaches to save costs by assessing the talents needed for their clubs or college programs online and from the comfort of their desks or homes.

The Problem

The product was designed to look into three basic issues that involves solving problems, creating opportunities, and making dreams a reality;

Solving Problems

We give professional clubs, college coaches, scouts, and agents access to game video of available players in their immediate environment or worldwide without leaving their desk

Creating Opportunities

We create job opportunities for skilled video freelancers to offer their services to players looking to create recruiting videos

Making Dreams a Reality

We provide opportunities for footballers worldwide to connect with clubs and coachers to take their careers to the next level

Competitive Analysis

User Interview and Surveys

What I did:
At the discovery phase of my project, I conducted user interviews and surveys in order to get a better understanding of the problem and required findings.

  • 1. How long have you been playing football?
  • 2. Where do you look forward to enhance your career?
  • 3. What’s your coaching ability?
  • 4. Have you been able to lead a team before, If yes, How?
  • 5. What are the challenges you face as a young footballer?
  • 6. How technology inclined are you?
