Bulk buying for traders

For users that want to buy a lot of NFTs at once, we introduced a "sweeping" tool that allows users to purchase up to 50 NFTs in one go. After we launched this, there was a substantial increase in daily trading volume. We had 145% increase in trading volume the day after launch

Minting on JPG Store

No developer is required to use our minting service. Collection owners will be able to set up mint phases directly in JPG without any coding involved. This got 700k ADA in minting revenue since launch

Website revamp using the Flowbite design system

Noticing how slow the process was for engineers to build custom components. Between three designers, we did an overhaul of our entire website and mapped it to the Flowbite design system in order to make implementation faster for engineers. Ensured there was a dark and light mode version for all pages. This sped up engineering + design workflow by at least 50% with lots of appreciation from our engineering team

Minting calculator

Price transparency is important in web3. We found that there weren't a lot of minting services that showed the final cost before sign up, so in order to gain trust and hopefully increase conversions, we added a free cost calculator right on the landing page. This increased minting conversion rate by 30%
