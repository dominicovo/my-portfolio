About

I am a UX Designer and fitness enthusiast, with 4 years of experience in Design (UX Research, UX Analysis and UI/Visual designs). I have extensive experience leveraging UX research methods and industry-leading tools to understand the product life cycle. As a UX Designer, I have designed and shipped products for companies which involves quantitative and data-driven applications.


Also having keen understanding of market dynamics that impact the successful launch of applications, I collaborate in a cross-functional environment and build consensus through influences as a recognized subject matter expert in various digital projects. Being a UX Designer for me means being involved in each phase of the design process from discovery, requirements analysis, visual design strategy & ideation, feature specification, process flow, user flows, wire-flows, prototyping to user testing and scaling.
